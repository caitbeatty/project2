Philadelphia:By Neighborhood

This website is designed to serve as resource for anyone interested in moving to the city of Philadelphia. The information presented here is specifically designed to address factors that apply to young families and individuals early in their careers. The census tracts are used as a backdrop for this analysis, providing a logical demarcation between different areas of the city. According to the Census Tracts 2010 data, the city of Philadelphia has 384 distinct census tracts.
